Crystal Hansen, BA., Fine Art

Born

Ontario in the 70s where lakes are blue and sand is pale, visual impression on color formed. Cerulean blue vs prairie sky blues.

The hill terrain and maple leaves lasting for weeks on trees as they turn from yellow, orange then red before falling. It is a treasured sight to befall.

Pursuits

Fascination and yearning to understand is at the heart. Printmaking coupled with photography for the negative/positive view. The core of focus. Digital print, internet and web design blossomed with new technologies further explored through mobile develepment and application development.

Code, language, machine learning, data, design became primary focuses. Data makes me squeal with excitement. Unfolding details with information. Research at the core of application. Coding became a new way to create and embody imagination.

Art in particular, photography,fashion, beauty, scultping dresses, furniture occupy me secondary endeavors. Each of these explorations uncovers new insights for transference.
