Crystal Hansen, BA., Fine Art

Born

Born in Ontario, Crystal experienced Canada multi-cultural and the vast geographical area of northern Ontario. Roots in the foundations of Computer Science, due to her fathers professional experience in Computer development of the time. Her creativity had started developing through witnessing soldering computer parts to motherboards before ever touching one.

The decision to focus on art was difficult. Being creative meant what does one do with their lives when fascination and yearning to understand is at its heart. Inspired by Mucha, printmaking was appealing as was photography with its negative view. These two fields are the core of focus on media. Multi-media and digital print houses became the career focus for a few years until the internet became accessible and drove new technologies.

B.A. Fine Art major focused on photography.
